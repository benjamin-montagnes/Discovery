Explore the boundaries of our solar system. Explore your universe.


Choose the shuttle which will lead you to the end of the world.
Move the arrows to move the spaceship. Move the mouse to look around you and point where you are going.
You will be able to modify the speed of your ship in the setting tab.
Press the spacebar to pause the game and access the settings.
